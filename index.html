Welcome to The Accessibility for Manitobans Act online learning portal!

 

The purpose of this online learning portal is to help Manitoba employers and organizations meet the minimum requirements for employee training required by the accessibility standards under The Accessibility for Manitobans Act (AMA).

Featured Learning Modules

This learning portal features modules, videos, and other resources for individuals and groups to learn about The AMA’s accessibility standards.

For other learning options and to provide your feedback, visit accessibilitymb.ca/contact. Stay tuned for future additions!
